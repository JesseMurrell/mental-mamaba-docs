Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Mental Mamba?
Mental Mamba combines classic Snake gameplay with math challenges. Control your snake to eat food by solving math problems correctly. Wrong answers will cost you a life! Use swipe gestures to control direction.
What happens when I lose all my hearts?
When you lose all hearts, the game ends and your score is submitted to the leaderboard. Hearts regenerate over time, or you can earn them through achievements and daily bonuses.
How do I create an account?
Open the app and tap "Sign Up" on the main menu. You can create an account with your email or sign in with Apple/Google. Having an account lets you save progress and compete on leaderboards.
Can I play offline?
Yes! Mental Mamba can be played offline. However, you'll need an internet connection to sync your progress, access leaderboards, and earn some achievements.
How do difficulty levels work?
Choose from Easy, Medium, and Hard difficulty levels. Higher difficulties feature more complex math problems, faster snake speed, and better score multipliers.
Is Mental Mamba free to play?
Yes! Mental Mamba is free to download and play. The game includes optional ads to support development. You can remove ads with a one-time purchase.
My progress was lost. Can you help?
If you were logged in to your account, your progress should sync automatically. Try logging out and back in. If the issue persists, contact support with your username and device info.
When will Android version be available?
We're actively working on the Android version! Follow us on social media or check back here for updates on the release date.
